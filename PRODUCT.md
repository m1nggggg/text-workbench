# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Developers, QA engineers, and system administrators comparing raw text or JSON configurations. They need a fast, local, private, and precise tool that works entirely in their browser.

## Product Purpose
To provide an elegant, developer-grade diffing utility that makes spotting differences in JSON structures and general text lines effortless.

## Current Capabilities

- Paste, type, drop, or open JSON and text files without uploading content.
- Compare JSON structurally with normalized object order and aligned arrays.
- Compare text by line with inline word/character detail and exact newline handling.
- Optionally ignore whitespace or case while preserving the original rendered text.
- Format or copy inputs, swap sides, clear content, and load examples without leaving the workspace.
- Keep large comparisons responsive by moving them to a cancellable background worker.

## Brand Personality
Precise, clean, developer-focused, fast.

## Anti-references
SaaS cliches, side-stripe borders, excessive padding, over-complicated settings, or noisy alerts.

## Design Principles
- Precise Hierarchy: Use distinct weights and oklch tinting rather than heavy borders or side stripes.
- Quiet & Dark-First: Tinted dark neutrals with single accent role ≤10% to prevent eye strain during long debugging sessions.
- Direct & Minimalist Copy: Use actions like "Beautify" and "Clear" instead of repetitive side descriptions.
- Results Are Evidence: Show paths, line numbers, before/after values, summary counts, and comparison duration.
- Never Stale: Any input mutation invalidates previously rendered results.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA, support high-contrast visual indicators for diff states (red/green/yellow) with distinct icons or clear typographic context, and respect reduced-motion.
